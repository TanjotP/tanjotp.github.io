Why Qi?

Healing is not just a cognitive practice, a neural practice, an inner-world “vipassana” meditative practice, but is a somatic and physical practice.

Living with qi, means being mindful of our dynamics, movements, posture, ergonomics, and breathing from finding awareness in mundane activities like walking and standing, to our fascia and eye muscles while learning a complex task that requires full focus.

Qi is a lifestyle, one of wu wei “without effort”. Living effortlessly.

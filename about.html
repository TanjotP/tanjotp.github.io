About me

My lore

I grew up waking up at 6am on Saturdays to build crafts and submit them to TV competitions. I built anything from a pulley-system elevator, to an entire civilization of figurines, and a minature greenhouse.

It was in Adobe Flash CS6, where I created and uploaded "miniclips-style" games for the world to play. In highschool, I tinkered around with NXT robotics and arduinos, creating all kind of projects like a camera gimbal stabilizer.

It was only a matter of time until my IOT driven projects needed mobile applications to connect with via. Bluetooth and WIFI.

In 2017, I developed my first ever android application in Xamarin at my first intership during university. From there I was hooked and never looked back since. I have spent the past 7 years witnessing the mobile application space evolve, and I am even more curious to see where it goes.

The first time I learned an algorithm was because I actually needed to use a sorting algo. in one of my 2 dimensional canvas-based games. I'm happy it was a genuine introdcution.


Present day

I'm passionate about mental health and personal self development as a service to the world + people around us. Outside of coding and designing, I enjoy teaching dance classes in Toronto and sharing my knowledge in mindful movement.
